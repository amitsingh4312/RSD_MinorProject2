Desserts open doors, hearts, and conversations. Desserted Island is the leading innovator in the dessert Industry. Though our reach is global, our passion for artful food and dedication to quality remains the motivation behind every one of our desserts. We challenge ourselves to aggressively source the best ingrediens. Among many milestones, we have eliminated eggs from key ingredients, use only hormone-free dairy and creams and removed artificial flavorings while developing house-made, natural alternatives.

Established in 1971, Desserted Island has never compromised with the quality and hygiene of the products sold at its frenchies. We take the feedback with a smile on our face and close to our heart. The company started with 23 dessert items when we started, and now over 500 items are sold in different areas.

#Tip of the Day

Candy is made by dissolving sugar in water or milk to form a syrup, which is boiled until it reaches the desired concentration or starts to caramelize. Candy comes in a wide variety of textures, from soft and chewy to hard and brittle. The texture of candy depends on the ingredients. Thus lower is the flame, smoother is the candy!
