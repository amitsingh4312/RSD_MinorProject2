About Us

In the winter of 1971, while serving as working in a cafe, Zach, Aujla, Moosewala and Josh met and became friends, sharing a love of cakes, smoothies, and burritos. With the help of their friends at 'The Immortal Cafe', they formed Desserted Island, a diverse, hand-cooked, hygienic, make-it-from-scratch, do-it-together creative bakery cum cafe, in a room they built above the shop’s kitchen.

In the years since, DI has worked on a wide array of unique desserts with some of the best people from the Pacific Northwest and beyond. Now we have 58 frenchise all over the world with best bakers for your service. We strive to keep our desserts unique, real and tasty, with room for a little magic. You can find us almost anywhere, and if your tongue hugs our dessert, we’d love to bake for you. Feel free to drop us an email to know more about us or book us for special arrangements.
